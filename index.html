About Me

Power Systems Engineer with extensive experience in transmission planning, SCADA/EMS systems, protection coordination, and renewable energy integration. Technical background combines power system analysis, electromagnetic transient simulation, and machine learning-driven approaches for grid stability prediction, vulnerability assessment, and automated power system workflows using Python.

Expertise in PSS/E modeling, protection relay coordination, IEC 61850 communications, and synchrophasor applications. Experienced with utility-scale renewable interconnection studies, NERC compliance analysis, and substation automation design. Contributed to projects supporting grid modernization, reliability improvement, and clean energy integration.

PhD research focused on power system dynamics and electromechanical wave propagation modeling, applying data-driven approaches to stability analysis and grid monitoring. Currently working on SCADA/EMS platform development and advanced grid automation applications at Siemens Energy in San Jose, CA.
