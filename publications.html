Research Areas

Selected Publications

Doctoral Dissertation

Electromechanical Wave Propagation Analysis

S. Yarahmadi, Virginia Tech, 2024

Analysis of rotor angle deviation propagation as electromechanical waves throughout power systems, developing advanced modeling techniques for dynamic stability assessment in modern grids with high renewable penetration.

Journal Publications

A Robust Data-driven Process Modeling Applied to Time-series Stochastic Power Flow

P. Algikar, Y. Xu, S. Yarahmadi, L. Mili
IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, Vol. 39, Issue 1, pp. 693-705, 2023

Develops robust data-driven modeling techniques for time-series stochastic power flow analysis, addressing uncertainty quantification challenges in power systems with high renewable penetration. Published in flagship IEEE power systems journal.

Conference Publications

Electromechanical Wave Propagation for Disturbance Arrival Time Assessment in Power Systems

S. Yarahmadi, P. Algikar, L. Mili
IEEE Power & Energy Society General Meeting (PESGM), 2023

Presents methodology for assessing disturbance propagation timing through power grids using electromechanical wave theory, supporting enhanced situational awareness and protection coordination.

Identification of Power System Oscillation Modes using Blind Source Separation based on Copula Statistic

P. Algikar, L. Mili, M.B. Hassine, S. Yarahmadi, A.M. Boker
IEEE Power & Energy Society General Meeting (PESGM), 2023

Proposes high-order blind source identification algorithm based on copula statistic for identifying oscillation modes in power systems, supporting enhanced wide-area monitoring and situational awareness.

Electromechanical Wave Propagation Analysis in Power Systems

S. Yarahmadi, P. Algikar, L. Mili
IEEE PES Generation, Transmission and Distribution Conference and Exposition, 2022

Introduces fundamental concepts of electromechanical wave propagation modeling for power system dynamic analysis, establishing framework for continuum approximation approaches.

A Novel Continuum Approximation to Power System Electromechanical Dynamics

S. Lei, D.A. Maldonado, E. Constantinescu, J. Zhao, S. Yarahmadi, L. Mili, M. Anitescu
IEEE PES Innovative Smart Grid Technologies Conference (ISGT), 2022

Develops novel continuum modeling approach for large-scale power system dynamics, enabling computationally efficient analysis of grid-scale phenomena.

Preprints & Working Papers

S. Yarahmadi, D.A. Maldonado, L. Mili, J. Zhao, M. Anitescu
arXiv preprint arXiv:2311.12326, November 2023

Introduces second-order nonlinear hyperbolic EMW propagation equation model accounting for voltage variations, addressing limitations in prior models for dynamic stability analysis in modern power systems.
