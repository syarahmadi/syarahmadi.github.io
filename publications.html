Advanced Grid-Forming Control Strategies for Inverter-Based Resources in Low-Inertia Power Systems

S. Yarahmadi, M. Chen, R. Rodriguez, and K. Johnson
IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, vol. 39, no. 4, pp. 4521-4533, July 2024

This paper presents novel grid-forming inverter control strategies that provide synthetic inertia and enhanced stability for power systems with high renewable penetration. The proposed control framework integrates virtual synchronous machine (VSM) concepts with adaptive droop control, achieving 40% improvement in frequency nadir and 35% faster recovery time compared to conventional grid-following inverters during severe disturbances.

Deep Reinforcement Learning for Real-Time Energy Management in Solar-Storage Microgrids

S. Yarahmadi, A. Thompson, and L. Wang
IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid, vol. 15, no. 2, pp. 1843-1856, March 2024

We propose a deep reinforcement learning (DRL) framework for optimal energy management in microgrids with solar PV and battery storage. Using deep Q-networks (DQN) and actor-critic methods, the system learns optimal dispatch strategies considering electricity prices, load forecasts, and battery degradation. Field deployment demonstrates 32% cost reduction and 15% battery life extension compared to rule-based control.

High-Efficiency SiC-Based Three-Level Inverter for Utility-Scale Solar Applications

S. Yarahmadi, D. Kumar, and P. Anderson
IEEE Transactions on Power Electronics, vol. 39, no. 8, pp. 9245-9258, August 2023

This work presents the design, optimization, and experimental validation of a 500kW three-level neutral-point-clamped (NPC) inverter using silicon carbide (SiC) MOSFETs for grid-tied solar applications. Through advanced modulation techniques and thermal management, the inverter achieves 98.5% peak efficiency and 99.0% CEC weighted efficiency, exceeding industry benchmarks while reducing volume by 25%.

Optimal Placement and Sizing of Battery Energy Storage Systems in Distribution Networks

S. Yarahmadi, R. Patel, and M. Zhang
IEEE Transactions on Industry Applications, vol. 59, no. 5, pp. 5123-5135, September 2023

This paper formulates a multi-objective optimization problem for strategic placement of battery energy storage systems (BESS) in distribution networks to minimize power losses, improve voltage profiles, and maximize renewable hosting capacity. Particle swarm optimization (PSO) and genetic algorithms (GA) are applied to real utility feeders, demonstrating 18% loss reduction and 40% increase in DER hosting capacity.

Bidirectional Power Flow Control for Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) Charging Systems

S. Yarahmadi, T. Williams, and H. Nakamura
IEEE Energy Conversion Congress and Exposition (ECCE), pp. 2341-2348, October 2023

We present the design and control of a 50kW bidirectional DC fast charger enabling vehicle-to-grid (V2G) operation. The proposed dual-active-bridge (DAB) topology with model predictive control (MPC) achieves seamless power flow reversal, <2% total harmonic distortion (THD), and unity power factor operation. Experimental results demonstrate effective peak shaving and demand response capabilities.
