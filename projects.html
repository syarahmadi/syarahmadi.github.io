Technical Projects

Power Systems Engineering & Grid Modernization Portfolio

Representative projects demonstrating technical capabilities across transmission planning, protection systems, renewable energy integration, SCADA/EMS, and grid modernization. Projects span utility-scale applications, consulting engagements, and research collaborations.

Transmission System Protection Coordination

Performed comprehensive protection coordination studies for high-voltage transmission substations. Conducted short-circuit analysis using ETAP and ASPEN software to determine fault current levels at various system locations. Developed time-current coordination curves for distance relays, differential relays, and overcurrent protection devices ensuring selective fault isolation per IEEE C37.2 standards. Validated relay settings for SEL, GE Multilin, and ABB IEDs through protection simulation and tested proper coordination under N-1 contingency scenarios.

ETAP ASPEN Relay Coordination IEEE C37.2 Short-Circuit Analysis

Utility-Scale Renewable Energy Interconnection

Supported interconnection studies for solar and wind generation facilities connecting to transmission and sub-transmission systems. Performed steady-state power flow analysis using PSS/E to evaluate voltage regulation, thermal loadings, and system losses. Conducted dynamic simulations to assess transient stability and voltage ride-through capability of inverter-based resources. Modeled generic renewable energy system models (REGC_A, REEC_B) per NERC requirements and evaluated compliance with IEEE 1547 grid support functions including volt-var and frequency-watt control.

PSS/E IEEE 1547 NERC Standards IBR Modeling Dynamic Simulation

SCADA System Design & IEC 61850 Implementation

Contributed to design and configuration of substation automation systems using IEC 61850 communication protocols. Developed substation configuration language (SCL) files defining intelligent electronic device (IED) data models, logical nodes, and GOOSE messaging for trip and status signals. Configured DNP3 and Modbus communication drivers for integration with master SCADA platforms. Implemented real-time data acquisition sequences and alarm management logic supporting operational monitoring and control applications.

IEC 61850 DNP3 GOOSE Messaging SCADA Systems Substation Automation

Grid-Scale Energy Storage Integration Analysis

Participated in technical analysis for battery energy storage system (BESS) integration into transmission and distribution networks. Evaluated energy storage applications including frequency regulation, voltage support, and energy arbitrage. Performed power flow studies to assess voltage impacts and thermal constraints during charging and discharging cycles. Analyzed state-of-charge management strategies and developed operational control sequences for grid service provisioning. Modeled BESS inverter controls and grid-following/grid-forming capabilities using PSCAD electromagnetic transient simulation.

BESS Analysis PSCAD Frequency Regulation Grid Services Inverter Controls

Load & Renewable Generation Forecasting

Developed probabilistic forecasting models for load demand and renewable generation using statistical and machine learning approaches. Implemented time-series models including ARIMA, exponential smoothing, and gradient boosting machines for day-ahead and hour-ahead predictions. Integrated numerical weather prediction (NWP) data including temperature, solar irradiance, and wind speed forecasts. Applied quantile regression techniques to estimate forecast uncertainty bounds supporting reserve requirement calculations and risk-aware dispatch optimization.

Time-Series Forecasting Machine Learning Python/MATLAB Probabilistic Methods Weather Integration

Synchrophasor-Based Grid Monitoring Applications

Contributed to research and development of wide-area monitoring applications using phasor measurement unit (PMU) data. Processed high-resolution synchrophasor measurements (60 Hz sampling) for real-time grid state visualization and event detection. Applied signal processing techniques including wavelet transforms and Hilbert-Huang transform for transient analysis. Developed automated classification algorithms using supervised learning to identify fault events, capacitor switching, and transformer energization from voltage and current phasor time-series data.

PMU/Synchrophasor IEEE C37.118 Signal Processing Event Classification Wide-Area Monitoring

Power Quality Analysis & Harmonic Mitigation

Performed power quality assessments for industrial facilities and distribution networks with high penetration of nonlinear loads and inverter-based resources. Conducted harmonic analysis using frequency scan methods to identify resonance conditions and evaluate total harmonic distortion (THD) levels. Designed passive and active filtering solutions for harmonic mitigation and reactive power compensation. Evaluated capacitor bank sizing and placement strategies to improve power factor while avoiding adverse harmonic interactions per IEEE 519 standards.

Power Quality Harmonic Analysis IEEE 519 ETAP Filter Design

Microgrid Design & Islanding Control

Supported technical design and control strategy development for microgrid systems capable of grid-connected and islanded operation. Analyzed load profiles and renewable generation characteristics to optimize distributed energy resource (DER) sizing using HOMER software. Developed seamless transition control logic for islanding and reconnection events maintaining frequency and voltage stability. Designed hierarchical control architecture including primary droop control, secondary frequency/voltage restoration, and tertiary economic dispatch optimization.

Microgrid Design HOMER Islanding Control DER Integration Black Start
